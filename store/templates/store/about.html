The art is to find the extraordinary in the ordinary and the ordinary in the extraordinary.

- Denis Diderot

The story of opening the gallery began when we started going to different exhibitions after different artists started coming to our schools and sharing their experiences with us. The art they showed us became an example that you can do wonders with just one brush. So we decided to look for similar artists, both famous and not. We started to create a selection of paintings and that's how we decided to open the gallery. Every painting has a story and a meaning. And we would like to bring this art into your lives.
